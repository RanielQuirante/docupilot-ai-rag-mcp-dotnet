DATA RETENTION & COMPLIANCE POLICY

Policy Number:   POL-COMP-007
Version:         3.1
Effective Date:  January 1, 2026
Policy Owner:    Office of the Chief Compliance Officer
Review Cycle:    Annual (next review: January 2027)
Applies To:      All employees, contractors, and systems handling company data

1. PURPOSE
   This policy establishes how Northwind Manufacturing, Inc. classifies,
   retains, archives, and securely disposes of business records in order to
   meet legal, regulatory, and contractual obligations and to minimize risk.

2. SCOPE
   This policy covers all records regardless of format (paper, electronic,
   email, and documents stored in the enterprise document platform), including
   contracts, invoices, employee records, and operational documentation.

3. RECORD CLASSIFICATION
   Records are classified into four categories, each with its own handling
   rules: Public, Internal, Confidential, and Restricted (PII / regulated).
   Employee records and anything containing personally identifiable
   information are classified Restricted by default.

4. RETENTION SCHEDULE
   - Contracts and legal agreements:   7 years after expiration or termination
   - Financial records and invoices:   7 years after the close of the fiscal year
   - Employee personnel records:       7 years after end of employment
   - Compliance and audit logs:        5 years
   - General correspondence:           2 years

5. SECURE DISPOSAL
   Upon reaching the end of its retention period, a record must be securely
   destroyed: electronic records by cryptographic erasure or secure deletion,
   and paper records by cross-cut shredding. Disposal events must be logged.

6. ACCESS CONTROL & AUDITING
   Access to Confidential and Restricted records is granted on a least-privilege
   basis. All access to and actions on regulated records must be auditable.
   Automated systems that act on documents must record an audit trail of every
   action they take.

7. LEGAL HOLD
   When litigation or an investigation is reasonably anticipated, the normal
   retention schedule is SUSPENDED for affected records until the hold is
   lifted by the Legal department. Destruction of records under legal hold is
   strictly prohibited.

8. ENFORCEMENT
   Violations of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and
   including termination, and may expose the company to legal and regulatory
   penalties. Questions should be directed to compliance@northwind.example.
