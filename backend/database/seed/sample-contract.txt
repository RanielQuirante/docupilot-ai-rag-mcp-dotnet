This Master Service Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between:

  Client:   Northwind Manufacturing, Inc.
            1200 Industrial Parkway, Columbus, OH 43215
  Provider: Brightline Cloud Solutions, LLC
            55 Market Street, Suite 900, San Francisco, CA 94105

1. SCOPE OF SERVICES
   The Provider shall furnish managed cloud-hosting, monitoring, and 24x7
   technical support services for the Client's enterprise document platform,
   as further described in the attached Statement of Work (SOW-1).

2. TERM AND EXPIRATION
   This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and shall remain valid
   until June 30, 2026 (the "Expiration Date"), unless earlier terminated
   in accordance with Section 6. The initial term is twenty-four (24) months.

3. RENEWAL
   This Agreement will NOT automatically renew. To continue services beyond
   the Expiration Date, the parties must execute a written renewal at least
   sixty (60) days prior to expiration. Absent a signed renewal, all services
   cease on the Expiration Date. The renewal deadline for the current term is
   therefore May 1, 2026.

4. FEES AND PAYMENT
   The Client shall pay a recurring service fee of USD $18,500 per month,
   invoiced monthly and due net thirty (30) days from the invoice date.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY
   Each party shall protect the other's Confidential Information using no less
   than reasonable care and shall not disclose it to third parties without
   prior written consent.

6. TERMINATION
   Either party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty
   (30) days' written notice if the breach remains uncured. Upon termination,
   the Provider shall return or securely destroy all Client data within
   fifteen (15) days.

7. GOVERNING LAW
   This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
